Sabrina Rinaldi sometimes accepts commissions.

To request a quote, please specify as much details as possible, such as desired width/height, how many models should be portrayed, close-up or full figure, backgrounds, etc.

Corners are not included.

You need to provide good quality pictures (close-up and full figure) of the model(s) to represent.

A 30% deposit is required. Balance should be payed at delivery.

It usually takes up to 4 weeks for the artist to complete the commission, depending on the difficulty level.

For further details, please get in touch via email.
